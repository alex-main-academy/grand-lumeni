How to prepare for laser hair removal?

IMPORTANT to follow the professional's recommended visit schedule, as well as all the rules of laser hair removal.

  • 10-14 days before the procedure

    Avoid visiting a solarium or direct sunlight

  • 14 days before the session

    Do not take tetracycline group antibiotics, as well as drugs related to fluoroquinolones.

  • 2-3 weeks before the procedure

    Exclude all other hair removal methods except shaving.

  • 3 days before the procedure

    Do not use alcohol-containing lotions/tonics

  • At least 10 hours before the procedure

    Carefully shave the areas where hair needs to be removed (do not apply any creams, deodorants, etc. on the shaved areas)
